ALL ABOUT DEANNA

My name is Deanna Johnson. I live in Kalispell Montana which is near the west entrance to Glacier National Park. I enjoy reading, playing games, watching movies and exploring ghost towns.

Currently I work in the shipping/receiving department for a small herbal remedy company. Previously I've worked as:

  • a merchandiser for Nabisco
  • a flood insurance processor
  • a dog kennel attendant
  • a shelver at a library
  • a customer service rep at a tech support call center.

I grew up in Southern California. I've been married for 33 years and have raised 5 beautiful daughters.

I'm now taking a turn in my career path and have decided to learn to code. I don't have any experience as yet but I am in the process of taking courses at Codefellows. Im excited for the adventure to come!
